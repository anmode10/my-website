Welcome to the World of Gases!

Explore the fascinating properties and uses of different gases like Oxygen, Nitrogen, Argon, and Carbon Dioxide.

Oxygen (O₂)

Oxygen is essential for life. It makes up about 21% of Earth's atmosphere.

Nitrogen (N₂)

Nitrogen is the most abundant gas in Earth's atmosphere, making up around 78% of it.

Argon (Ar)

Argon is a noble gas, and it is colorless, odorless, and inert.

Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)

Carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas and is vital in the process of photosynthesis in plants.
